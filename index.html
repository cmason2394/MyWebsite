About

Hi there! This is my test-trial into the world of coding. I am interested in programming and data analysis, and this web page is a way to learn and practice coding, while keeping track of where I am and where I want to go. I am exploring building websites, designing apps, and analyzing data.

Projects

  • Website design

    Learn how to create visually appealing and user-friendly websites. Potentially design websites professionally.

  • App development

    Learn how to create apps! Design a riddle app, where you could find riddles, get hints when you are stuck, and upload your own riddles.

  • EveryDollar visual representations

    I use a personal finance app called EveryDollar. It is great, except it doesn't have visual representations of how I allocate and spend my money. I want to learn how to grab the numerical data from the app and show it in graphs and charts to better understand my finances over time.

  • Energy Use visual representations

    My mom relies entirely on solar and battery storage for energy use at her house. She wants a visual representation of real-time energy use without going through the internet so that other people can't access her data.
